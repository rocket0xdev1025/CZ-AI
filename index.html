How did the story begin?

Inspired by the cultural impact of Changpeng Zhao, this project is an AI-driven blockchain meme experiment that blends internet humor with autonomous agents. It uses AI personas to generate memes, narratives, and on-chain interactions, turning community sentiment into evolving digital culture.

The project does not claim affiliation, but reimagines the CZ archetype as a symbolic figure representing decentralization, resilience, and builder mentality in crypto.
